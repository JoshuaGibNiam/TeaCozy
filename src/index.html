Tea Cozy

Brewing coziness in tea since 1992

Founded in 1992, our tea brewing company has been dedicated to the art of crafting exceptional teas for over three decades. We carefully source quality leaves and brew with patience and precision, honoring traditional methods while refining our process over time. Every cup reflects our commitment to balance, flavor, and the quiet pleasure of well-made tea.

Jasmine Tea

Our jasmine tea is crafted through a traditional multi-scenting process, where fine tea leaves are gently layered with fresh jasmine blossoms to capture a refined, lingering floral aroma. The result is an elegant, balanced cup that reflects our commitment to patience, quality, and craft.

Green Tea

Our green tea is carefully crafted from tender leaves, gently processed to preserve freshness, clarity, and a naturally balanced flavor.

Black Tea

Our black tea is fully oxidized for depth and character, delivering a smooth, robust cup refined through careful sourcing and precise craftsmanship.

Milk Tea

Our milk tea is blended for balance and smoothness, pairing quality tea with a refined creaminess for a rich yet clean finish.

Chinese Tea

Our Chinese teas honor regional traditions, selected from trusted origins and crafted using time-tested methods that highlight purity and terroir.

Tea Leaves

Our loose-leaf teas are selected for freshness and integrity, allowing each leaf to fully unfold and express its natural complexity.

Teabags

Our teabags use whole-leaf quality tea, thoughtfully packaged for convenience without compromising aroma, flavor, or balance.

Our premium recipes, perfected with three decades' experience, detailed with subtle nuances and colourful flavours. All in one place, aligned with our vision: to serve the public.
